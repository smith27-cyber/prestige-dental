About Us

Welcome to Prestige Dental Clinic, a family-run dental practice established in 2016. Located in Kawuku, 14 kilometers from Entebbe and opposite Abujja Shopping Centre, we are committed to offering comprehensive general dental care with a focus on preventive dentistry.

Our practice is patient-centered, ensuring that every individual receives long-term care aimed at achieving the best possible treatment outcomes. By using the latest dental technologies, we aim to make a lasting, positive impression on all our patients.

Our Services

  • Consultation and Examination
  • Root Canal Therapy
  • Simple and Surgical Extractions
  • Teeth Cleaning
  • Teeth Whitening
  • Dental Fillings
  • Crowns and Bridges
  • Orthodontic Treatments (Braces and Aligners)
  • Mouth Guards
  • Veneers
  • Full and Partial Dentures
  • Dental Implants
  • Onlays and Inlays
  • Treatment for Jaw Fractures

At Prestige Dental Clinic, your dental health and satisfaction are our top priorities. Whether you need routine care or specialized treatment, our skilled team is here to provide the highest level of care in a warm and welcoming environment.
